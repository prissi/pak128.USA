<h1>Lines:</h1>
Lines are useful to control large numbers of vehicles that serve the same route. You can also give them a personalized name to help identify them.<br/>
Lines can be managed from the <em>Line Management</em> window, which can be accessed from the top toolbar or pressing the <em>"w"</em> key.
</p>
<p>
<h1>First steps:</h1>
<em>[1]</em> First click on the <em>garage</em> at {pos} using the <em>'{tool1}'</em> and buy a <em>{bus1}</em> bus.<br/>
<em>[2]</em> You need to configure a <em>Line</em> to manage multiple vehicles at once. Click on the <em>Serves line</em> filter and then on <em>Create new line</em>. This opens the <em>Line Window.</em>
</p>
